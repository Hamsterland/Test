# Plural Form

### Information

When pluralizing acronyms, do not use an apostrophe before the _**s**_.

{% hint style="info" %}
Pay close attention to whether some acronyms can be pluralized by adding an _**s**_ to the end. When in doubt, Google is your friend.
{% endhint %}

### Examples

**Correct**

UFOs

**Incorrect**

UFO's
